of oak are our ships hearts of oak are our men we always are ready steady boys steady well fight and well conquer again and again we never meet our foes but we wish them to stay they never meet us but they wish us away if they run then we follow and drive them ashore for if they wont fight us we cannot do more hearts of oak are our ships etc monsieur thurot in the absence of boyce went over to
